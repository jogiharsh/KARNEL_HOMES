Our Process

Your journey from vision to keys.

A clear, ten-step process that takes you from first conversation to moving day — with transparency at every stage.

01

Initial Meeting

We discuss your ideas, budget, timeline, and property — whether it's empty land, a knockdown-rebuild, or a custom build.

02

Site Check

We review your block's size, slope, and local council rules before starting the design to ensure everything is possible.

03

Design Choice

Pick one of our pre-designed floor plans or work with us to create a custom layout that fits your lifestyle.

04

First Estimate

We give you an early cost estimate based on your chosen layout, style, and land details so you know your budget early on.

05

Detailed Quote

You get a clear, jargon-free proposal outlining your exact design, included features, site costs, and final pricing.

06

Plans & Paperwork

Our team prepares all the official paperwork, including architectural drawings, engineering reports, and energy assessments.

07

Council Approval

We handle the approval process for you (DA or CDC), managing the paperwork and talking to the certifiers.

08

Style & Selections

Personalize your home by choosing your colors, materials, fixtures, and finishes with help from our design team.

09

Construction

Watch your home come to life as we manage the entire build, from pouring the concrete slab to the final coat of paint.

10

Handover & Care

We do a final quality check, walk you through your finished home, hand over the keys, and provide ongoing maintenance support.
